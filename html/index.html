Main Features

  • Show your friends what you're listening to by using /now-playing
  • Search for tracks, artists, and albums with /track-search, /search-artist, and /search-album
  • Get detailed information about any track, artist, or album
  • View your top tracks, artists, and albums from Last.fm
  • Discover similar artists and tracks
  • View global and country-specific music charts
  • Track your weekly listening habits
  • See your loved tracks and scrobbles

Get Started

To start using Musica Bot, simply invite it to your Discord server and use the /login command to connect your Last.fm account. Once authenticated, you can start exploring all the amazing features!

Musica Bot integrates seamlessly with Last.fm to bring you the best music discovery and sharing experience on Discord.
